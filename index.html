What makes nota different

01

Offline first, always

Everything lives on your device first. Sync happens in the background when you're online — your notes never depend on a server being up.

02

Real Markdown

Not a WYSIWYG with hidden markup. Plain Markdown that renders beautifully — export to .md anytime, no lock-in.

03

E2E encryption

Your notes are encrypted on-device before sync. Not even we can read them. Zero-knowledge architecture with open-source crypto primitives.

How it works

01

Write in plain Markdown

Open a note and start typing. Use # for headings, **bold**, - lists. The editor stays out of your way — no toolbars, no menus.

02

Encrypted before it leaves

When you save, nota encrypts your note with your key before touching the network. Your passphrase never leaves the device.

03

Syncs quietly in background

Come back online and changes merge seamlessly across all your devices — with conflict resolution that prefers your latest edit.

04

Export anytime, own your data

Export everything as .md files or a .zip archive. Your notes are yours — readable in any text editor, forever.
